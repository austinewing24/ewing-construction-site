Our Story

Started in 2014, Still Family-Run Today

Ewing Construction was founded in 2014 as a family operation, and it has stayed that way ever since. We're based in Davis County, Utah, and build custom homes and commercial projects across the Wasatch Front, but every project still runs through the same family that started this company.

That matters because construction is a trust business. When you hire Ewing Construction, you're not handed off to a rotating cast of project managers. You work with Austin and Paul directly, from the first conversation about your lot to the final walkthrough of your finished home.
